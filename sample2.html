ILAHIA COLLEGE

Mulavoor

Ilahia Trust made a leap forward in the educational field by pioneering one of the first self-financing engineering colleges.
Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET), Mulavoor P.O., Muvattupuzha is a co-educational minority institution approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Kerala Technological University (KTU).
